Who are we?

Study On is an organization founded by students to help students. Our volunteers come from a variety of fields and all have different experiences they would love to share. We were originally founded in Saint Barbara's Youth Ministry (SBYM) program. Since 2020, we have been our own independent organization, however, we still use the facilities and resources that SBYM provides.

Three Main Focuses

College Applications

  • Application Workshops
  • Essay Days

Study Skills

  • AP Testing
  • Study Halls
  • Tutoring

Professional Development

  • Resume Revision
  • Interview Preparation

Student's Testimonials

Pre-Pharmacy Student at UCI

Hello! My name is Emily. I am currently a first year student at UCI in the pharmaceutical sciences department. I had a great experience with interacting with all these amazing mentors. They really helped me through the college application process. As the only child, I did not have a lot of people to go to for help regarding applying to colleges. Even though from the outside the events they held might not seem significant, they really lifted a huge amount of stress and burden college applicants felt. From giving personal insights to holding many workshops, these guys really gave great tips and guidance. When I first heard about their first workshop, I thought it was of no use to me and that I would not learn anything that I did not know before. However, by the end of the night, I had learned a lot about the application process that I did not know before. Attending that workshop gave me new insights and allowed me to make connections. I was able to meet a mentor who had a major that I was interested in, and they were more than happy to give me some insight regarding that major. These mentors are experienced and knowledgeable, and they really have the students at the best of interest.

- Emily Dang

College Initiative, 2019
